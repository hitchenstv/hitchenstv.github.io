The CN WoW equivalent for CurseForge is [WCLBox](https://www.wclbox.com/). They have an addon-manager called NewBeeBox that now supports English. It's also in this program you'll find WoWSims for Titan Reforged.

### Essential AddOns:
* BiaoGe This is Gargul on steroids for GDKPs. BiaoGe handles all GDKP bids, healer/tank bonuses, fines and calculates splits. It allows you to browse available gear from raids and to set wishlists to get notified when the items you want drop.

* MeetingHorn
